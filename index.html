001
Consistency doesn't matter if you're consistently wrong.

Repetition amplifies the direction you're already moving. Make sure the direction is right before you commit to it.

002
The market you're in matters more than how good you are.

A great operator in a shrinking market loses. Position matters more than execution. You can't outwork a structural disadvantage.
